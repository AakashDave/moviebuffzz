About

I created this website using online movie api.You have to search any movie & series in a search box & related data will be shown on dashboard.

So,It's an online database containing information and statistics about movies, TV shows and video games as well as actors, directors and other film industry professionals.
This information can include lists of cast and crew members, movie release dates and box office information, plot summaries, trailers, actor and director biographies and other trivia.

Information on IMDb & OMDb comes from a variety of sources, such as filmmakers, film studios, on-screen credits and other official sources.

However, much of the information comes from users themselves, who can submit facts in a wiki-style format.

Unlike traditional wiki sites, it always authenticates information before it appears online -- although errors do show up, and the website allows users to report possible mistakes so they can be fixed.
